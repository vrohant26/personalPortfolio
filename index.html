FAQ

01. What Services Do you offer ?

Our services include web design, development, website management, and SEO. We focus on creating visually appealing, functional websites that perform well, remain current, and rank higher in search engine results.

02. How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on requirements. Once we have all the necessary information, basic design typically takes 1 week, and development around 2 weeks, totaling approximately 4-5 weeks.

03. What do you need from me to start a project?

To get started, I need details about your business, including content and images if you have them. If not, I can create content and visuals for you. Your own content can often be more relatable, but I’m happy to collaborate and create together if needed.

04. How much does a project typically cost?

Project costs depend on the website type and specific requirements. We offer services for various website types, including e-commerce, business, personal, blog, and portfolio sites. Contact for a personalized quote tailored to your needs!

05. Can I pay you in installments ?

Yes, payments can be split into four installments: an initial payment before design, one after design, another before development, and the final payment after development completion.
